ABOUT THE ARTIST

Hi! I'm Nat Castagno

Nat Castagno is an artist based in the Springfield, MA/Hartford, CT area. She is an abstract artist known for her bright color palettes and use of texture. Nat is inspired by color, textures, and interior design. She teaches monthly virtual painting classes.
